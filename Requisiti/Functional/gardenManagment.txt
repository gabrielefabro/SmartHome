1. Irrigation Management:
    Program watering based on times, days or weather conditions
    Ability to manually start and stop irrigation through the system interface

2. External Lighting Management:
    Control of external garden lights
    Support scheduled turning on/off of lights to improve safety

3. Environmental Sensor Monitoring:
    Automatic adjustment of watering and lighting in response to environmental conditions

4. Gate Management:
    Remote control of the gate and use of sensors based on the proximity of a magnetic key
